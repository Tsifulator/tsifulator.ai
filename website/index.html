How it works

From question to finished model

tsifl is a sidebar inside Office. You talk to it; it does the work.

01

Install the add-in — 60 seconds

Open Excel or PowerPoint, go to Insert → Add-ins, search "tsifl." One click installs the sidebar into every Office file on your machine. No IT department needed.

02

Tell it what you're building

"Build a comp table for MSFT, GOOGL, META with EV/EBITDA and P/E." tsifl fetches live data, calculates multiples, and writes structured cells — handling blanks, errors, and formatting automatically.

03

Iterate in plain English

"Add a football field chart." "IB format the headers." "Flag anyone above 25x." tsifl understands your context and makes surgical changes without touching anything else.

04

Hit Build Deck → open PowerPoint

When your analysis is ready, one click queues the slides. Switch to PowerPoint and tsifl populates every slide automatically — titles, data tables, commentary. Save and send.

FAQ

Common questions

How is tsifl different from Macabacus or Excel's built-in AI? ⌄
Macabacus gives you keyboard shortcuts. Excel Copilot gives you generic AI. tsifl is agentic — it understands the financial context of what you're building, pulls live external data, reasons about your model, and acts across multiple apps simultaneously. You don't configure shortcuts; you describe what you want.
Where does the market data come from? How accurate is it? ⌄
Live price and market cap data comes from Polygon.io (previous close). Fundamentals — revenue, EBITDA, earnings, margins — come from Financial Modeling Prep (FMP) with yfinance as a fallback. Data is the same as public financial APIs. For LBO-grade accuracy, we recommend verifying against your data provider.
Does it work on Windows and Mac? ⌄
Yes. The add-ins run inside Microsoft Office's JavaScript runtime, which is cross-platform. Excel and PowerPoint add-ins work on Mac and Windows. The R add-in requires the tsifl desktop agent running locally.
Is my data private? Does tsifl see my spreadsheets? ⌄
tsifl only reads the data you explicitly send via the chat interface. When you click "Build Deck," tsifl reads the active sheet to generate slide content. Nothing is stored permanently — only what's needed to complete the task. We never train on your financial data.
What happens after the 14-day trial? ⌄
You'll be prompted to subscribe at $99/month. If you don't, the add-in switches to a limited free tier (500 tasks/month). You won't lose any of your work — just access to unlimited tasks.
Can I cancel anytime? ⌄
Yes. Cancel from Stripe's customer portal anytime. No cancellation fees. If you cancel mid-month, you keep access until the period ends.
